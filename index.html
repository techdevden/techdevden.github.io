About Me

Hi there! My name is Denis, and I am a professional software developer with over seven years of applied industry knowledge and experience. I started off my career working for a small startup located in Fort Lauderdale, FL. While there, I gained tons of experience working in a startup environment where resources were limited, but innovation was abundant.


I then transitioned to working for a digital advertising technology company named Sonobi, Inc. located in Winter Park, FL. During my time at Sonobi, I worked on their proprietary software named JetStream. Some of my daily tasks consisted of writing code, troubleshooting (i.e., bugfixes, hotfixes, etc.), and conducting peer code reviews just to name a few. Sonobi played a paramount role in my career because it was there where I further enhanced my skills working on software, gained the knowledge and experience working in agile teams, and understood the overall development and sprint lifecycles.


As I continued to progress in my career, I accepted a contractual opportunity with American Express Company located in Sunrise, FL as an Engineer II. While there, I worked on Amex OneApp and was a part of two teams which were the Dashboard team and the Secure Messaging Center team. The Dashboard team focused on React UI components and analytics integration for both domestic and international user markets. The Secure Messaging Center team focused on one-way messaging from Amex to its customers by using several business functions and Java.


Fast-forward to the present since no longer being with Amex, I started my own tech channel named TechDevDen out my passion for uplifting disenfranchised communities through technology. My aim is to empower individuals with the knowledge and skills to create anything imaginable.
